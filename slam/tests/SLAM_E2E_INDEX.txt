================================================================================
WORKFLOW AT A GLANCE
================================================================================

Step 1: Build SNP mask from 0h sample
        Input: WDHD1-0h-3 FASTQ
        Output: wt0.mask.bed.gz

Step 2: Detect trims from 6h sample
        Input: ARID1A-6h-1 FASTQ
        Output: trim5p, trim3p (from QC JSON)

Step 3: Run STAR-SLAM on 0h with mask + trims
        Output: 0h_SlamQuant.out, 0h.bam

Step 4: Run STAR-SLAM on 6h with mask + trims
        Output: 6h_SlamQuant.out, 6h.bam

Step 5: Run GEDI on both BAMs (optional)
        Output: 0h.tsv.gz, 6h.tsv.gz

Step 6: Compare STAR vs GEDI
        Output: compare_0h.txt, compare_6h.txt

Total time: ~40 minutes
Disk space: ~50-100 GB
